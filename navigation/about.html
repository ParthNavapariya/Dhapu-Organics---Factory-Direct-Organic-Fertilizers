JOURNEY

Our Journey

1

Founded

The Vision for Soil Health

Dhapu Organics Pvt. Ltd. was established with the mission of empowering farmers and restoring soil health. With the belief of being a true 'Farmers' Companion', the company focused on reducing the harmful impact of chemical fertilizers by providing high-quality bio-fertilizers and cost-effective solutions to improve crop yield and sustainability.

2

Growth

Diverse Agricultural Solutions

As the company grew, it expanded its product range to include 50+ specialized agricultural solutions across organic manures, bio-fertilizers, micronutrients, NPK fertilizers, pesticides and growth regulators. These innovations were designed to enhance plant health, improve immunity, and optimize overall crop productivity.

3

Today

A Trusted Brand in Indian Agriculture

Today, Dhapu Organics has become a trusted name among thousands of farmers across India. Its solutions support major crops like wheat, cotton, sugarcane, and vegetables, helping improve soil fertility, crop quality, and long-term sustainable farming practices.
